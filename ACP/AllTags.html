Nature

Nature is the connection between the physical world surrounding us and the life inside us. Nature is God's most precious and valuable gift to humans. It is the principal source of all essential nutrients for all living things on the planet.


Importance of Save Nature
Protecting ecosystems: Saving nature protects forests and other natural ecosystems.
Reducing the effects of climate change: Green spaces can help reduce the risk of damaging floods and slow water flow down.
Improving health: People who live close to nature are more likely to be social and experience less crime.
